Shipping

Get Your Package Ready

▪ Pack your items in a sturdy box.

▪ Seal your box with plastic or nylon tape at least two inches wide. Don’t use duct tape.

▪ Wrap items separately and use cushioning material.

▪ You’ll need to know the dimensions and weight of your box.

Create a Shipping Label

▪ Choose how fast you want your package to arrive.

▪ See how much it will cost.

▪ Pay by card or PayPal, or pay in store at a UPS location.

We’ll Do the Rest

▪ We can come to you (for an additional fee)

▪ Drop it off at a UPS location. Be sure to bring your identification.

▪ Hand the labeled package to your friendly neighborhood driver.
